​​​Public planning meetings​

Make sure you're aware of the following points in respect of public planning meetings:

  • agendas are available five days prior to each meeting date
  • all applications will have an associated officer report - these are listed below the agenda items
  • use the 'search' function in Adobe Reader in order to locate the item on the agenda or minutes that you wish to view
  • minutes are available after they have been agreed and signed off
  • hard copies of minutes can be obtained from the States Greffe  
  • if you require copies of agendas and minutes dated prior to the dates displayed on this page, contact the Planning Administration Team
